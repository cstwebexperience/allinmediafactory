# ALL IN MEDIA - Idei de Design

## Context
Website premium pentru agenția de marketing ALL IN MEDIA. Trebuie să fie ultra-modern, cu animații fluide, orientat pe conversii, cu temă dark și accente neon (albastru/mov). Include secțiuni: Hero, Despre Noi, Servicii, Colaborare, Rezultate (screenshots), Video (YouTube Shorts), CTA, buton WhatsApp flotant.

## Idea 1: "Neon Brutalism"

<text>
**Design Movement:** Neo-Brutalism meets Cyberpunk — raw, bold geometric shapes combined with neon glow effects on a deep dark canvas.

**Core Principles:**
1. Extreme contrast between massive typography and delicate neon accents
2. Asymmetric, off-grid layouts that break conventional alignment
3. Layered depth through overlapping elements and glassmorphism cards
4. Raw energy expressed through sharp angles and bold borders

**Color Philosophy:** Deep charcoal (#0A0A0F) as the void, electric cyan (#00F0FF) as primary energy, hot magenta (#FF00AA) as accent pulse. The colors represent digital power and creative disruption.

**Layout Paradigm:** Broken grid with overlapping sections. Content blocks float at different z-levels. Sections use diagonal clip-paths and staggered columns.

**Signature Elements:**
- Glowing neon borders that pulse on hover
- Oversized numbers/stats with gradient text fills
- Noise/grain texture overlay on dark backgrounds

**Interaction Philosophy:** Aggressive hover states — elements scale, glow, and shift. Scroll triggers dramatic reveals. Cursor leaves a subtle trail effect.

**Animation:** GSAP-style staggered reveals, parallax depth layers, counter animations for stats, magnetic button effects.

**Typography System:** Clash Display (bold display) + Space Grotesk (body). Extreme size contrast — hero titles at 6-8rem, body at 1rem.
</text>

## Idea 2: "Cinematic Dark Luxury"

<text>
**Design Movement:** Cinematic Luxury — inspired by high-end film production studios and premium brand experiences like Apple's product pages.

**Core Principles:**
1. Cinematic storytelling through scroll — each section is a "scene"
2. Restrained elegance with strategic moments of visual drama
3. Content-first hierarchy with generous breathing room
4. Premium feel through subtle gradients and refined micro-interactions

**Color Philosophy:** True black (#050507) as the stage, deep indigo-to-violet gradient (#2D1B69 → #7B2FBE) as the signature brand gradient (matching the ALL IN MEDIA logo), warm white (#F5F0EB) for text. The palette evokes exclusivity and creative authority.

**Layout Paradigm:** Full-viewport cinematic sections with horizontal scroll for galleries. Content centered with wide margins. Alternating between immersive full-bleed sections and contained content blocks.

**Signature Elements:**
- Smooth gradient orbs floating in the background (subtle, animated)
- Stats displayed as large cinematic counters with a film-grain overlay
- Screenshot gallery with perspective tilt on hover (3D card effect)

**Interaction Philosophy:** Smooth, intentional — elements fade and slide into view as if being revealed on a stage. Hover states are subtle but satisfying (slight lift, glow). Everything feels deliberate.

**Animation:** Framer Motion spring physics for entrances, smooth scroll-linked parallax, counter animations with easing, staggered card reveals, subtle background gradient shifts.

**Typography System:** Syne (bold, geometric display) + DM Sans (clean body). Display at 4-6rem with tight letter-spacing, body at 1.125rem with comfortable line-height.
</text>

## Idea 3: "Digital Flux"

<text>
**Design Movement:** Generative Digital Art meets Swiss Design — clean structural grids infused with dynamic, flowing visual elements.

**Core Principles:**
1. Structured chaos — rigid grid foundations with fluid, organic overlays
2. Data-driven aesthetics — stats and metrics become visual art
3. Movement as identity — constant subtle motion creates a living interface
4. Monochrome base with strategic chromatic accents

**Layout Paradigm:** Modular grid system with cards that morph and rearrange. Masonry-style results gallery. Sections separated by animated SVG wave dividers.

**Signature Elements:**
- Animated mesh gradient backgrounds that shift colors slowly
- Stats displayed inside rotating 3D card faces
- Horizontal infinite scroll ticker with client results

**Color Philosophy:** Jet black (#0D0D0D) base, electric blue (#3B82F6) as the data color, lime green (#84CC16) as the growth/success accent. Colors represent technology, growth, and precision.

**Interaction Philosophy:** Playful and responsive — cards tilt toward cursor, buttons have spring animations, scroll triggers cascading reveals like falling dominoes.

**Animation:** CSS scroll-driven animations, infinite horizontal ticker, 3D perspective transforms on cards, morphing SVG shapes between sections.

**Typography System:** Outfit (geometric, modern display) + Inter (neutral body). Numeric data uses tabular figures for alignment. Display at 3-5rem, body at 1rem.
</text>
